Report a Found Item

Tell the community what you found so it can make its way back to the right person.

Optional but highly recommended. Clear photos help owners identify items.

Submissions are reviewed by staff before appearing in the public list.

Tips for Accurate Reports

  • Include specific details: color, brand, unique marks, stickers, or labels.
  • Avoid sharing sensitive information (like student ID numbers) publicly.
  • Drop the item at your school’s official lost-and-found location after submitting.
  • Check the “Browse Items” section periodically to see what’s already posted.

What Happens Next?

  1. Staff review your request and compare your description with the item details.
  2. If it looks like a match, you receive instructions on where and when to pick it up.
  3. You may be asked to show ID or answer verification questions in person.
